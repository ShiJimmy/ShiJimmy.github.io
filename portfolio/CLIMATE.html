Climate Apartheid

As a person who is chasing equality and living in the area that suffers from extreme weather events such as typhoons and thunderstorms every year, I am interested in how different countries deal with the more and more extreme weather events caused by climate changes. This map is focusing on climate-driven displacements, highlighting the disproportionate burden that poorer countries bear due to climate change; while also emphasizing the minimal responsibility they have for the pollution causing these impacts.
