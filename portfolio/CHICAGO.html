Chicago Public Transportation Analysis

This project analyzes the relationship between land value and public transportation accessibility in Chicago. Using spatial data and statistical methods, the study explores how proximity to transit stations and routes influences property values across different neighborhoods. The findings provide insights for urban planners and policymakers to optimize transit investments and support equitable development.
